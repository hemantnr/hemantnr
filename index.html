Hemanth N R

I am a graduate student in Materials Science and Engineering at the University of Washington, where I work with Sol-Gel group advised by Prof. Guozhong Cao. Here, I study lithium ion battery (LiBs) degradation for frieght trucks.

Currently, I work at Electric Hydrogen as a R&D co-op with Cell Modelling team.

Prior to UW, at Log9 Materials, I've worked on split cell, coin cell and 18650 cell formats. I specialized in LTO slurry chemistry development for LiBs and investigated degradation mechanisms in supercapacitors. In the past life, I graduated from National Institue of Technology Karnataka Surathkal, India in 2021, where I majored in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering. Here, I worked with Prof. Narayan Prabhu on paraffin wax for thermal energy storage.
